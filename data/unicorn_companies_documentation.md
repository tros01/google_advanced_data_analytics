# Unicorn companies dataset

- dataset available on [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/learn/go-beyond-the-numbers-translate-data-into-insight/resources/9mSWv)
- **1,074 rows** (companies)
- **10 columns**

| column name | type | description |
|-------------------|----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Company | str | Company name |
| Valuation | str | Company valuation in billions (B) of dollars |
| Date Joined | datetime | The date on which the company reached $1 billion in valuation |
| Industry | str | Company industry |
| City | str | City the company was founded in |
| Country/Region | str | Country the company was founded in |
| Continent | str | Continent the company was founded in |
| Year Founded | int | Year the company was founded |
| Funding | str | Total amount raised across all funding rounds in billions (B) or millions (M) of dollars |
| Select Investors | str | Top 4 investing firms or individual investors (some have fewer than 4) |

Data made available by Coursera for the [Google Advanced Data Analytics Professional Certificate program](https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/google-advanced-data-analytics).
